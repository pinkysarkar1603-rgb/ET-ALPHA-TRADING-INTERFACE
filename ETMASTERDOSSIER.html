ET Alpha closes that gap by combining live data, technical indicators, AI reasoning, event context, and interactive visual analysis inside one product surface.

Core Product Areas

A multi-surface product instead of a single flat dashboard

The strongest thing about ET Alpha is how many research layers it consolidates without losing clarity. Each surface has a distinct job, but together they form a full market-analysis loop.

Markets

Live technical signal workspace for ticker scanning, conviction scoring, trend labeling, support and resistance, and AI-generated stock summaries.

Info

Candlestick-first research surface for move blueprint, trade-map framing, and contextual explanation tied directly to visible price action.

Intel

External-driver layer that separates raw price movement from domestic, global, sector, and company-linked context.

Radar

Workflow-oriented workspace for compare, events, rotation, watchlists, alerts, impact analysis, and broader thematic exploration.

Bharat Intelligence

India-first natural-language research and screening for users who want easier discovery than rigid screener filters.

DATA

A dedicated indicator lab with searchable indicator catalog, spotlighting, fullscreen chart mode, and overlay-driven technical research.

Why this structure works

Users can move from scanning to explanation to comparison without switching products or losing context between tabs.

Why it feels richer

The product combines dashboard utility, chart analysis, AI interpretation, and event research into a more serious market workflow.

Why it is catchier

Instead of a plain terminal-like experience, ET Alpha leans into visually expressive research surfaces that still map to practical use.

Operational Snapshot

The build is also grounded in a practical working stack

Beyond the concept, ET Alpha has a clear implementation story across frontend, backend, live data flows, and AI integration. That matters because it makes the project feel credible, not merely aspirational.

Frontend

React, TypeScript, Vite, Framer Motion, and Recharts create an interface designed for responsive visual research and polished user flows.

Backend

FastAPI and indicator computation utilities power the market data layer, aggregation routes, and signal logic behind the product.

AI Layer

Groq-powered analysis supports technical narratives, explanation flows, and additional reasoning layers tied to market context.

Data Orientation

Yahoo Finance endpoints and internal indicator engines contribute live and computed context rather than static placeholder experiences.
